ReferenceSystem Guide
🎯
Own It Completely
You are the single source of truth. If it's not updated here, it doesn't exist. No one else is responsible for this data — you are.
⚡
Update in Real Time
The moment a line moves, log it. Don't batch updates. One update now saves ten confused phone calls later.
📅
Think 3 Weeks Ahead
Always have upcoming shows populated before anyone asks. Prep beats reaction every time.
🔔
Flag Before It's a Problem
A line marked "overdue" is a failure. A line flagged "due soon" is a success. Anticipation is your superpower.
🤝
Communicate Proactively
Don't wait to be asked. Brief, routine check-ins eliminate the confusion that comes from silence.
📋
Notes Are Your Proof
Short, dated notes on each line movement cost 10 seconds and save hours of argument.
RoutineYour Weekly Rhythm
MONReview all line statuses. Confirm anything due back this week. Check upcoming shows within 2 weeks and verify assigned lines.
WEDMid-week check: any lines overdue? Any new show requests? Update locations if anything has moved.
FRIEnd-of-week sweep. Log any movements. Confirm return dates for lines expected Monday. Flag anything unaccounted for.
SHOWSPre-show: confirm lines are packed and accounted for. Post-show: log returns within 24 hours.
ALWAYSIf someone asks where a line is, you should know without hesitating. If you don't, update your system right now.
